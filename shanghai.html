Some Must Visit Locations

The Oriental Pearl Tower

Ascend to new heights of wonder at the Oriental Pearl Tower, where a soaring architectural marvel offers panoramic views of Shanghai's glittering skyline, leaving you breathless and spellbound by the city's boundless energy and futuristic allure.

Yu Garden

Indulge in a captivating retreat within the exquisite realm of Yu Garden, where traditional elegance and serene landscapes intertwine, transporting you to a sanctuary of tranquility amidst the vibrant energy of Shanghai.

The Bund

Experience the mesmerizing fusion of history and modernity as you stroll along the iconic Bund in Shanghai, where stunning architecture, dazzling lights, and the majestic Huangpu River create an electrifying and lively atmosphere.

The Jade Buddha Temple

Discover an oasis of serenity and marvel at the captivating jade statues within the Jade Buddha Temple, a spiritual haven in Shanghai.

The Shanghai Museum

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of ancient art and culture at the Shanghai Museum, where extraordinary treasures and masterpieces unveil the rich tapestry of China's heritage.

Nanjing Road

Embark on a thrilling shopping adventure at Nanjing Road, Shanghai's ultimate paradise of retail bliss, where endless options, vibrant ambiance, and a dynamic atmosphere converge to create an unforgettable experience for every avid shopper.
