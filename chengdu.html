Some Must Visit Locations

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding, where you can witness the endearing charm of pandas up close and contribute to their conservation, creating cherished memories that will warm your heart for a lifetime.

Wenshu Yuan Monastery

Discover inner peace and immerse yourself in the serene beauty of Wenshu Yuan Monastery in Chengdu, where ancient traditions and tranquil surroundings create an oasis of harmony and contemplation.

Anshun Bridge

Step into history and marvel at the enchanting Anshun Bridge in Chengdu, where timeless architecture and the gentle flow of the Jinjiang River create a captivating setting.

Leshan Giant Buddha

Witness the majestic wonder of the Leshan Giant Buddha in Chengdu, an awe-inspiring masterpiece that embodies the power and serenity of ancient art and spirituality.

Mount Emei

Journey to one of the four most divine Buddhism Mountains in China, where misty peaks, stunning panoramas, and sacred temples converge to create a captivating landscape that will enchant your senses and ignite your spirit of adventure.

Dujiangyan Irrigation System

Discover the marvels of ancient engineering at the Dujiangyan Irrigation System in Chengdu, where ingenious waterworks harness the forces of nature, offering a captivating glimpse into the past and a profound appreciation for human ingenuity.
